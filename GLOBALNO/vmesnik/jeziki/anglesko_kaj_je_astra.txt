AstraMentalica appears to be a spiritual "sandbox" platform designed as an interactive, archetypal, and meditative ecosystem. Based on the project's documentation, it integrates various esoteric and creative elements into a structured interface for personal development and exploration.

Core Components of the Platform
Archetypal Entities: The system features six primary guardians, each representing different aspects of consciousness:

Stellarion (Cosmic Owl): Represents ancient wisdom and the poetic cosmic perspective.

Lunara (Silver Fox): Focuses on lunar cycles, intuition, and the subconscious.

Terra (Tree Deer): Grounding, nature, and shamanic connection.

Kryptus (Black Raven): Guardian of hidden knowledge and mysteries.

Aetherin (Light Butterfly): Pure energy and healing.

Numeron (Golden Snake): Guardian of sacred numbers and patterns.

Modular Systems: Users can toggle various modules including Tarot, Runes (ᚱ), Frequency/sound healing, and botanical wisdom.

Content Libraries:

Codex Damiris: An extensive encyclopedia covering archetypes, cosmic order, and symbolic systems.

Liber Aquae: A resource centered on emotional healing and navigating the subconscious.

Sound Gates (Zvočna Vrata): A repository for frequency-based therapeutic audio.

Sigil Cards: A visual toolset for meditation and intention setting.

Interactive Storytelling: The platform includes a specialized section for children’s stories designed to teach spiritual and ethical lessons, categorized by age and theme (e.g., courage, nature, inner strength).

User Interaction
The platform supports a "Voice Input" (Glasovni vnos) feature and a series of "Guardian" commands that allow users to navigate between sections like the library, rune sets, and frequency settings. It also includes a gamified progress system with milestones such as "First Step," "Rune Reader," and "Starry" (Zvezdni) levels, which track user engagement through conversations and exploration.
